My Life

By Suzanna L. Horton

I was born two months early on January 12, 1973 in Decatur, GA in the middle of a terrible ice storm.
I've since been 5-10 minutes late everywhere I go to make up for my early arrival.

I enjoy the following hobbies:

  • Brewing beer
  • Cooking
  • Collecting vinyl records
  • Reading
  • Traveling
  • Cheez-Its

Things I do not like:

  • English peas
  • Folding laundry
  • Waking up early
  • Atlanta traffic
